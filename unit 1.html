Unit 1

    ★  Human Values:

  • ➤    Human values refer to the fundamental principles or beliefs that guide human behavior, decisions, and actions. These values may include things like honesty, respect, responsibility, compassion, and fairness, among others. They are considered universal and timeless and are based on our shared humanity.

    • ★  Morals:

  • ➤    Morals refer to the principles or beliefs that guide an individual's behavior or actions. They are often based on cultural or religious values and may differ from person to person or society to society. Morals may relate to issues such as honesty, loyalty, justice, and compassion.

    • ★  Values and Ethics:

  • ➤    Values and ethics are closely related concepts. Values refer to the principles or beliefs that guide an individual's behavior, while ethics refer to the moral principles or standards that govern behavior in a particular profession or organization. Ethics are often based on shared values within a profession or organization, and they guide decisions and actions that align with the values of the group.

    • ★   Integrity:

  • ➤    Integrity refers to the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles. It involves consistently behaving in a way that is consistent with one's values and beliefs, even in the face of challenges or temptations.

    • ★  Work ethics:

  • ➤    Work ethics refer to the set of moral principles or values that guide an individual's behavior in the workplace. This may include things like being punctual, taking responsibility for one's actions, being honest, and working hard to achieve one's goals.

    • ★   Service learning:

  • ➤    Service learning is a type of educational approach that combines academic learning with community service. It is designed to help students develop a sense of social responsibility and civic engagement, while also gaining practical skills and knowledge.

    • ★  Civic virtue:

  • ➤    Civic virtue refers to the set of values and behaviors that are considered necessary for individuals to participate in and contribute to their communities and society as a whole. This may include things like voting, volunteering, and being involved in local government or community organizations.

    • ★  Respect for others:

  • ➤    Respect for others refers to the value of treating others with dignity, consideration, and understanding. It involves recognizing and valuing the differences between individuals and avoiding behaviors that are hurtful or discriminatory.

    • ★  Living peacefully:

  • ➤    Living peacefully refers to the value of promoting harmony and cooperation within communities and between individuals. It involves resolving conflicts in a nonviolent and constructive manner and working to build relationships based on mutual respect and understanding.

    • ★  Caring and Sharing:

  • ➤    Caring and sharing refer to the value of showing compassion and generosity towards others. It involves being willing to help others in need and sharing resources and opportunities to promote the common good.

    • ★  Honesty:

  • ➤    Honesty refers to the quality of being truthful and transparent in one's dealings with others. It involves avoiding deceitful or misleading behavior and being willing to admit mistakes or shortcomings.

    • ★  Courage:

  • ➤    Courage refers to the quality of being brave and taking action despite fear or uncertainty. It involves standing up for one's beliefs and values and taking risks to achieve important goals or make a positive impact in the world.
